# Prayer Time Web Application 🕌

A beautifully designed, modern, and highly accurate Prayer Times application. This application provides real-time prayer schedules, Islamic calendars, Qibla direction, and customizable notification systems tailored for Muslims around the world.

## 🌟 Key Features & How to Use

### 📍 Location & Prayer Times
- **Automatic Detection**: When you open the app, it automatically detects your city and displays accurate prayer times for your current location.
- **Manual City Search**: If you are traveling or want to check times for another city, click on the **Settings (⚙️)** icon and use the **Manual City Search** to type any city name (e.g., Mumbai, Dubai, London).

### 🕌 Jamaat Time Adjustment
- **Set Your Masjid's Time**: Not all Masjids pray exactly at the Adhan time. Click the **Pencil (✏️) icon** next to the next prayer, or go to Settings to manually input the exact **Jamaat time** (e.g., 05:30 AM) for your local Masjid. The app will display both the Adhan and Jamaat times.

### 🔔 Custom Notifications
- **Per-Prayer Alerts**: In the Settings menu, you can selectively enable notifications for specific prayers (e.g., only Fajr and Maghrib).
- **Sound Options**: Choose your preferred notification sound when it's time to pray:
  - System Default
  - Short Beep
  - Adhan (Makkah)

### 📅 Dual Islamic Calendars & Festivals
- Click the **Calendar (📅)** icon in the top navigation to view the monthly prayer schedule.
- Toggle between **English (Gregorian)** and **Urdu (Hijri)** calendars using the globe icon (🌐).
- **Islamic Festivals**: Important Islamic dates and festivals (like Eid-ul-Fitr, Ramadan start) are automatically highlighted on the calendar dates.

### 🧭 Qibla Compass
- Scroll down to the Qibla section to see the direction of the Kaaba from your location.
- **Enable Device Compass**: On mobile devices, click the button to activate your device's sensors. The needle will physically rotate as you turn your phone, guiding you directly to the Qibla.

### 🌙 Dynamic Themes & Duas
- The app's background automatically changes to reflect the current time of day (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night).
- A new Islamic Dua or motivation is displayed at the bottom of the page every day.

## 💻 Technologies Used

This application is built with a focus on speed, responsiveness, and a premium user experience without relying on heavy frameworks.

- **Core Structure**: HTML5
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS3 (Custom Glassmorphism design, CSS Grid, Flexbox, CSS Variables)
- **Logic & Functionality**: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
- **APIs Integrated**:
  - **Aladhan API**: Used for fetching highly accurate daily prayer timings, Hijri calendar conversions, and Islamic holiday data.
  - **IPAPI**: Used for fast, automatic IP-based user location detection.
- **Web APIs**: Geolocation API, Device Orientation API (for Qibla), Notification API, and HTML5 Audio.
